# Pascal's Mugging
Pascal's mugging refers to a [thought experiment](https://wiki.lesswrong.com/wiki/thought_experiment) in decision theory, a finite analogue of [Pascal's wager](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pascal's_wager).
